Who We Are

Home > About

My mission is to help you tour the world so you can make it a better place.

At YCT Travel, we specialize in small group adventure holidays and

travel tours for every kind of customer. Whether you're looking for a

solo travel experience, an active family adventure or a self-guided,

walking or cycling holiday, YCT Travel offer over 600 trips worldwide

in 130 countries.


Our variety of adventure travel holidays take our customers to all

corners of the globe. From group walking and trekking tours in Africa

and Asia to cycling trips in the Americas and polar voyages in

Antarctica and the Arctic, we also offer family adventure holidays,

trips for solo travellers and much more. Passion and experience are at

the heart of our ethos and we pride ourselves on over 36 years of

expertise as one ofthe most trusted adventure travel companies in

the world.

Never a dull moment

We create exciting itineraries that will take you off the beaten track to fully experience the culture of each destination. Our fully trained YCT Travel Leaders will also always be on hand to add a unique 'YCT Travel twist' to your trip.

We believe travel should change us, not the world. That communities and cultures should benefit and flourish and that we should leave nothing but footprints behind.

That is why responsible travel is at the core of everything we do, from sponsoring projects around the world and offsetting carbon emissions of our flights to supporting local communities and protecting wildlife.
